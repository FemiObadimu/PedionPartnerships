The Entrepreneurial Manager

The course for managers focuses on equipping them with skills that ensure the realisation of corporate objectives. The manager is made to realise his contribution to the bottom line from an entrepreneurial perspective.

Number of Days: 5

Modules:

  • Entrepreneurship -Vision
  • Mission
  • Goals
  • Entrepreneurship & The DNA of an 'Entrepreneurial Manager'
  • Understanding the value chain
  • (Understanding the “Big Picture” & effects of not delivering on promises)
  • Creating the Blue Ocean strategy
  • Brand Management
  • Teams – Developing Effective Teams
  • Problem solving
  • Business Decision Making

Benefits:

  • Mind-sets of participants will be influenced to be more entrepreneurial in their thinking & the impact of their actions on the bottom line & brand of the organisation.

Target Audience: Managers & Senior Supervisors
